Cavern
"While other tribes fought for the surface, we gathered strength in the dark."

Cavern is a tribe born from the depths. Millennia underground made their skin white and their minds sharp. They came into the light later than the others, but brought with them the knowledge of the deep that no one above possesses.

What Is
Vibe City

Vibe City is Zargates' gaming platform. The community builds the buildings, and each one is a separate AI product: games in different genres, services, and tools. A building generates turnover, and share holders receive their portion.

A building share = a percentage of all transactions inside it, forever. As the platform grows, your income grows with it. Payouts come in Stars and USDT.
How to Get Shares
Events: orcs with the right trait receive shares in a specific building. Golden orcs: shares drop automatically whenever a new building opens.

FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
What is Cavern and how is it connected to Zargates?▼
Cavern is one of the 18 tribes in Zargates. A tribe is a group of orcs united under one banner. Cavern is EPIC rarity, with white-skinned orcs and a rhino totem. It competes in the Battle of Tribes, takes part in events, and builds its presence inside Vibe City.
How do I get Vibe City building shares?▼
There are two paths: 1) Events — orcs with the right trait receive shares, as in the Ethereum event. 2) Golden orcs — automatic share drops whenever a new building opens. Winning the Battle also grants Grand Casino shares.
What does a box in Zargates give you?▼
Orcs from different tribes and a scratch card with Stars. The amount is random, and you only find out after opening it. The Premium Pass and the Horseshoe increase Stars and improve the chance of rare orcs.
Why Cavern if other tribes also have EPIC rarity?▼
EPIC rarity is the entry point, not the uniqueness. Cavern stands out because of its own identity (white skin, rhino), its presence in Vibe City, its event-focused community, and the content built around the tribe — stickers, guides, and merch.
How much does it cost to join?▼
Base entry is free. Warrior costs 500 Stars per season and includes event breakdowns, collection analysis, and the trading desk. Elite costs 2000 Stars per season and adds personal review, top-lineup placement, and the VIP channel.

Endless Session Loop
How A Run Works
01

Scout the Breach

The cave opens with one breach lane and several blocked pockets. The player reads the terrain, initial enemy tags, and first resource nodes.

02

Shape the Route

Before or between waves the player extends the path with pale stone blocks, trap corridors, fungal forks, and tribal gates.

03

Anchor Towers

Cavern towers are placed on carved platforms. Some are raw damage, some spread fear, some harvest spores, and some reward longer enemy travel.

04

Survive Forever

The run never ends. Enemies mutate, map rules evolve, and the player keeps rebuilding the route to hold the chief's undercity one wave longer.

Wave Doctrine
Enemy Progression Over Time
Stage 01

Scout Pressure

Fast Ash Runners and light swarms test whether the player understands route length, first choke-points, and early support towers.

Stage 02

Armor + Support

Siege Tuskers and Glow Leeches force target priority. The player must balance anti-elite pressure with control and splash damage.

Stage 03

Route Counterplay

Tunnel Burrowers and flying Spite Bats attack the current strategy itself, forcing rebuilds, anti-air layers, and alternate lane planning.

Stage 04

Ritual Siege

Boss entities like the Sun-Eater Pilgrim modify the map, mutate nearby enemies, and create rule-breaking waves that define the endless identity.
